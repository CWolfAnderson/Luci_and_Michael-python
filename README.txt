Luci & Michael

A story about Lucifer Morningstar being envious and wanting to have the Earth for himself and God sending Archangel Michael to protect the Earth from Satan.
Whose side will you choose? The side of heaven or the side of hell?

Run the game with:
    $ python main.py

You will need pygame installed

Lucifer's Controls:
    W, S, A, D     up, down, left, right respectively
    I, K, J, ;     shoot up, down, left, right respectively

Michael's Controls:
    UP, DOWN, LEFT, RIGHT       up, down, left, right respectively
    (numpad) 6, 3, 2, enter     shoot up, down, left, right respectively

The goal is to deplete your opponent's health before they deplete yours.

Game based on The Binding of Isaac
